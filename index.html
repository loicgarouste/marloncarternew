Born in Brooklyn, New York, Marlon Carter is an iconic brand inspired by the 90's New York vibe. Fuelled by the spirit of freedom and the looming Y2K apocalypse, style and expression was a mix of hip-hop, punk rock and the embryonic electro scene which permeated into mainstream pop culture. Marlon Carter feeds of the style, culture and art scene of the 90's New York scene bringing in it's own reinterpretation of the iconic frames worn by the such style icons as Marlene Kuntz, Afterhours, Vasco Rossi, Ligabue and a very young Fabrizio Moro.
